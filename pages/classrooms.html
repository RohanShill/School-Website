About Our Classrooms

Our school features well-designed, spacious classrooms that provide an ideal learning environment for students from Class 1 to Class 8. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids and comfortable seating arrangements to facilitate effective learning and student engagement.

We maintain a student-friendly atmosphere with proper lighting, ventilation, and safety measures to ensure that every child can learn in comfort and focus on their education.
