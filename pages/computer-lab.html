About Our Computer Lab

Our state-of-the-art computer laboratory provides students with hands-on experience in computer education and digital literacy. Equipped with modern computers and educational software, the lab serves as a gateway to the digital world, preparing students for the technology-driven future.

We focus on building fundamental computer skills, logical thinking, and problem-solving abilities through practical learning and interactive sessions.
