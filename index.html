About Our School

PM Shri Middle School Hiranpur Boys is a historic educational institution established in 1912, with over a century of excellence in education. We provide quality education from Class 1 to Class 8, nurturing young minds and building strong foundations for their future. Our school is proud to be recognized as a PM Shri School, showcasing the implementation of the National Education Policy 2020 and serving as an exemplar institution providing holistic, equitable, and joyful education.
